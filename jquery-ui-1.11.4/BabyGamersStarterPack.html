Want To Get Into The Industry?

We want more women in games! And women want to be in games! So what's stopping us?
Well, a lot the problem is that society doesn't exactly make it easy for us. But, no worries, because no matter your skill level, there are people who have made resources to help you.

  • Interested in developping but don't know how to code?
    Check out Code Academy! Lots of resources for learning how to code, which is essential to video game development. You can also take a look at W3Schools for more coding references.

  • Still in high school and looking for an intensive program?
    Girls Who Code has an opportunity that's right for you. Connect with peers and mentors to build an app and learn valuable skills in this immersive program.

  • Looking for opportunities as a developper?
    Game Dev Map shows your where the action is! If you know what you're looking for, don't be afraid to apply.

  • Want more opportunities specific to women in the games industry?
    Women in Games Jobs (WIGJ) is a network specifically for women interested in the industry; they'll hold panels, fairs, and other events, and are present in multiple countries, including the USA, India, Japan, France and China. Check out your local chapter, or contact them online!
